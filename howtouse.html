How can I listen to music?

  1. Click to button called (Music)
  2. You will come up with types of music such as (Mood, Focus, Coding and etc.)
    choose the one that you are interested in.
  3. After clicking one. You will come up with music players with their names on.
    Click triengle button to start the music.
  4. You can change the duration by moving the circle.
  5. You can increase/decrease volume of the music by putting the pointer to sound icon and control the volume.
